# Reservoir Performance and Water Production Analysis

## Overview

This repository contains an analysis of reservoir performance with a focus on water production behavior and recovery evaluation. The project examines key reservoir engineering concepts such as water coning challenges and material balance calculations to understand production trends and reservoir dynamics.

The objective of this study is to evaluate how water production affects reservoir performance and to apply analytical methods to estimate reservoir characteristics and recovery behavior.

## Objectives

* Analyze reservoir production performance.
* Study the effects of **water coning** on hydrocarbon production.
* Apply **material balance techniques** to evaluate reservoir behavior.
* Understand water production patterns and their impact on recovery.

## Analysis Performed

1. **Water Coning Challenge**

   * Investigation of water coning phenomena during production.
   * Identification of factors influencing water breakthrough.
   * Discussion of operational challenges caused by water production.

2. **Material Balance Analysis**

   * Application of material balance principles to estimate reservoir parameters.
   * Evaluation of reservoir pressure behavior.
   * Interpretation of reservoir drive mechanisms.

## Data and Tools

* Production data analyzed using **Microsoft Excel**
* Reservoir engineering calculations and plots performed in Excel

## Repository Contents

* Excel file containing production data analysis
* Calculations related to water production and reservoir performance
* Material balance evaluation
